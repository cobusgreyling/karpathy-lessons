# 9. The One-Page Summary

The whole guide, one line per chapter. (For the ten aphorisms, see the
[Ten-Line Distillation](index.md#the-ten-line-distillation).)

1. **[Training recipe](01-training-recipe.md)** — training fails silently; become one with the data, build an end-to-end skeleton, overfit before you regularize, change one thing at a time.
2. **[Common mistakes](02-common-mistakes.md)** — the framework will not warn you: `.zero_grad()`, train/eval mode, logits vs probabilities, a learning rate off by 10×. Correctness is your job.
3. **[Software 2.0](03-software-2.0.md)** — weights found by optimization are a new kind of code; the dataset is the source, and the tooling is still being invented.
4. **[Tesla data engine](04-tesla-data-engine.md)** — the flywheel is the product: ship, mine failures, label the long tail, retrain, repeat. The model is the easy part.
5. **[LLMs as a paradigm](05-llm-paradigm.md)** — the LLM is the kernel, the context window is RAM; intelligence is jagged, and much of the weirdness is tokenization.
6. **[Working with LLMs](06-working-with-llms.md)** — keep the loop tight, treat the model as a fast fallible junior, curate context, and pick your autonomy per task.
7. **[Learning and building](07-learning-and-building.md)** — build it from scratch once; understanding comes from re-deriving, not re-reading.
8. **[Education](08-education.md)** — the bottleneck is great explanation; AI is the teaching assistant, the human designs the path.
